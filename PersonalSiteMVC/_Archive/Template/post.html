My Work

Below are some examples of projects I built myself.

I'm a really big fan of the Destiny series by Bungie. So I put together a couple of projects themed around that universe, each project showing a different aspect of coding I've learned.'


Studio Project

 (DESTINY: CRUCIBLE CRASH COURSE) A static website designed to provide information about a local education service to improve your skills in Destiny's Crucible PvP.

Dungeon Project

 (DESTINY: LOST SECTOR v1) A dungeon crawler to root out the source of danger to the Last City. Set in the Destiny Universe, it is your duty to investigate the threat and put a stop to it. Current features include randomized encounters, stats that influence your precision, damage, and critical hits, as well as a companion that can save you in the event of death if you've built it's confidence up. Future mechanics include a narrative-driven adventure to discover the source of the enemy's power, mechanic-based combat, multi-enemy battles, and much more.
